# Brain Teaser Challenges (No Answers)

## 1. Bridge Crossing Puzzle
Four people must cross a bridge at night with one torch. Times: 1, 2, 5, 10 minutes. Only two can cross at a time. What is the minimum time for all to cross?

## 2. Lightbulb Switch Puzzle  
You have 3 switches outside a room and 3 bulbs inside. You can enter the room only once. How do you determine which switch controls which bulb?

## 3. Poisoned Wine Puzzle  
You have 1000 wine bottles, one is poisoned. The poison kills in 24 hours. You have 10 test subjects and 48 hours. How can you identify the poisoned bottle?

## 4. Wolf, Goat & Cabbage Puzzle  
A farmer must transport a wolf, a goat, and a cabbage across a river. Boat holds farmer + one item. Wolf eats goat, goat eats cabbage. How can all cross safely?

## 5. Counterfeit Coin Puzzle  
You have 9 coins; one is fake and lighter. Using a balance scale with no weights, what is the minimum number of weighings needed to identify the fake coin?

## 6. Two Eggs & 100 Floors Puzzle  
You have 2 eggs and a 100-floor building. Eggs break at some unknown floor F. Find F with the minimum number of worst-case drops.

## 7. Blue Eyes Island Puzzle  
100 people on an island have blue eyes. No one knows their own eye colour. A visitor says, “I see someone with blue eyes.” What happens next?

## 8. Rope Burning Puzzle  
You have two ropes that each burn for exactly 1 hour but burn unevenly. How can you measure exactly 45 minutes?

## 9. Missing £1 Puzzle  
Three people pay £30 for a room. They receive £5 back, keep £3 total, bellboy keeps £2. But 27 + 2 = 29. Where is the missing £1?

## 10. Chessboard Grain Puzzle  
You place 1 grain on square 1, 2 on square 2, 4 on square 3, doubling each time. How many grains are on the board in total?
